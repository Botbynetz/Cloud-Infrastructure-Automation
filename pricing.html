Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade anytime to unlock more features. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Any unused deployments don't roll over.
What happens after I use all my deployments?
You'll need to upgrade to a higher tier or wait until next month. We recommend the Enterprise plan if you deploy frequently. Ultimate tier has unlimited deployments.
Do I need my own AWS account?
Yes, you need to provide your AWS credentials. We deploy resources to YOUR AWS account, so you maintain full ownership and control. We never store your credentials.
What's included in the Free trial?
The Free trial includes 1 deployment of the Self-Service Portal module only. It's perfect for testing our platform before committing. No credit card required. Limited to one-time use.
Can I choose which modules in Professional tier?
Yes! Professional tier lets you choose any 3 modules from our 10 available modules. You can swap modules once per billing cycle.
What's the difference between Enterprise and Ultimate?
Ultimate includes ALL 10 modules (Enterprise has 7), unlimited deployments (vs 50), multi-cloud support (AWS+Azure+GCP), AI-powered auto-healing, 30-minute support response (vs 4 hours), and white-label option. Perfect for mission-critical production environments.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any tier! You only pay the monthly/annual subscription. AWS infrastructure costs are separate and billed directly by AWS to your account.
Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?
Yes! For organizations with unique requirements (100+ deployments/month, custom modules, dedicated infrastructure), contact our sales team for custom pricing.
