      FAQ

      Frequently Asked Questions

      How does token-based pricing work?

      You buy tokens upfront, then use them to deploy infrastructure modules. Each module costs a certain number of tokens based on complexity (1.0, 2.0, or 3.0 tokens). Configuration like production environment, multi-region, or high availability adds multipliers to the base cost.

      Example: Kubernetes (2.0) + Observability (1.0) in production (+20%) = 3.6 tokens = Rp 288.000

      Do tokens expire?

      No, tokens never expire! Buy them once and use them anytime. There's no time limit or pressure to use them quickly. This gives you complete flexibility to deploy at your own pace.

      What happens if my deployment fails?

      You don't pay for failed deployments! We only charge tokens when your deployment is successful and passes all health checks. If something goes wrong (AWS quota, configuration error, infrastructure failure), your tokens are automatically refunded.

      This is our "Success Guarantee" - you only pay for working infrastructure.

      When do I pay for deployments?

      The deployment process works like this:

      1. Configure & Start: Select modules and configuration - deployment begins immediately
      2. Deploy & Test: Infrastructure is provisioned and tested automatically
      3. Payment Gate: System pauses and shows final cost - you must pay to activate
      4. Activate: After payment, deployment goes live and becomes accessible

      You have 7 days to complete payment. If you don't pay, the deployment is saved as draft and resources are suspended (no charge).

      Can I buy more tokens later?

      Yes! Buy tokens anytime through:

      • Credit Card: Instant credit via Midtrans payment gateway
      • Bank Transfer: Credited within 1-2 hours after confirmation
      • E-wallet: GoPay, OVO, Dana, ShopeePay - instant credit

      Tokens are added to your balance immediately and can be used right away.

      How do multipliers work?

      Multipliers are applied based on your deployment configuration:

      • Production (+20%): Additional monitoring, safeguards, and validation
      • Multi-Region (+50%): Deployment across multiple AWS regions for redundancy
      • High Availability (+30%): Auto-failover, redundant resources, enhanced reliability
